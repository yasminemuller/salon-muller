Welcome to Salon Müller

Located in the heart of Grand Baie, Salon Müller is a peaceful retreat dedicated to your well-being. Specializing in massage, hair, and nails, our salon offers a luxurious experience in an elegant, vintage-inspired setting. Our mission? To enhance your natural beauty while giving you a moment of pure relaxation.

Our Services

  • Nails: Manicure, pedicure, polish, and hand care.
